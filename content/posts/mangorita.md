It's like a mango slushie, but it gets you buzzed.

Prep time: 5 minutes  
Yield: 2 large drinks

## Ingredients

- 2 jigger tequila
- 1 ounces cointreau
- 2 jigger lime juice
- 3/4 cup pineapple chunks
- 1 cup frozen mango chunks
- 3 cups ice

## Equipment

- a blender

## Instructions

1. Put all ingredients in the blender.
2. Blend on medium high until smooth.
